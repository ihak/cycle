A cycle is a special two wheel transportation machinery that is powered by humans. Its basic components include
1. A revolving handle to direct it into a particular direction.
2. A seat to sit on while cycling.
3. Two wheels, front and rear.
4. A pair of peddles.
5. Gears along with chain and breaking system.
6. Optinally a carieer or a basket or both to carry stuff.
7. Mud gards covering the wheels.
